## Streaming Cost Controls

You mentioned wanting to keep streaming cost-efficient. Here's the battle plan:

### Technical Safeguards

| Risk | Safeguard | Implementation |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| Runaway CDN costs | Viewer caps | Max concurrent viewers initially (1,000, then 5,000, etc.) |
| Storage bloat | Lifecycle policies | Move old content to Glacier after 90 days |
| Transcoding costs | Pre-encode everything | Don't use on-demand transcoding |
| DDoS/abuse | Rate limiting | CloudFront rate limits, WAF rules |
| Bandwidth spikes | Quality throttling | Reduce to 480p during peaks if needed |

### Cost Monitoring

```
Set up CloudWatch alarms:
- Alert at 50% of monthly budget
- Alert at 75% of monthly budget
- HARD STOP at 100% (temporarily disable new streams)
```

### Scaling Thresholds

| Viewer Milestone | Action |
|------------------|--------|
| 100 concurrent | Monitor only |
| 500 concurrent | Review costs weekly |
| 1,000 concurrent | Implement quality tiers |
| 5,000 concurrent | Consider dedicated infrastructure |
| 10,000+ concurrent | You've made it—invest in proper CDN contract |
